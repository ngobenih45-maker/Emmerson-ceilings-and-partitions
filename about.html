Who We Are

Emmerson Ceilings & Partitions is a professional home improvement company based in Ebony Park, Midrand. We specialize in providing high-quality ceiling installations, drywall partitions, suspended ceilings, bulkheads, aluminum work and interior finishing for residential, commercial and industrial properties.

We are committed to delivering excellent workmanship, reliable service and outstanding customer satisfaction. Whether you are renovating your home, building a new office or upgrading your commercial property, our experienced team is ready to bring your vision to life.

Our Mission

To provide professional ceiling and partition solutions that combine quality workmanship, affordability and exceptional customer service.

Our Vision
